Payment
We accept Payment from all Major Indian Bank through NetBanking, Debit Card, Credit Card and PayUmoney Wallet.

Procedure for Purchasing a Product through NetBanking, Debit Card, Credit Card :-

  • Register your self in our website www.electronicscomp.com if you are not registered, other wise login with your account details.
  • Add the Products into cart that you want to buy.
  • Click on the cart button on top right corner and then in cart click on Checkout after that you will be redirected to Checkout page.
  • Enter all the details and select the Shipping option.
  • In Payment Section, Select Debit Card, Credit Card and NetBanking option and accept the Terms & Conditions, then click on Confirm Order Button, you will be directed to payment gateway page.
  • There are three modes one is Card, 2nd is NetBanking and 3rd is Wallet, select the mode from which you want to pay.
  • In Card mode, select Debit Card or Credit card whichever you are using for payment and enter card details and click on Pay Now Button.
  • In NetBanking mode, Select the bank from the list and click on Pay Now button.
  • In Wallet mode, Select the Wallet from the list and click on Pay Now button.
  • After payment, You will be redirected back to our site and order status will be displayed.
  • Check the status of your order in Order history tab in My Account section.
